Frequently Asked Questions

How long does UK LTD formation take? ▼

Usually 24–48 working hours after document submission. We handle all Companies House filing and paperwork on your behalf.

Do I need to be a UK resident? ▼

No — we assist non-UK residents with UK LTD formation. You'll need a valid passport and proof of address from your home country.

What documents do I need to provide? ▼

Valid photo ID (passport or national ID) and proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or government letter dated within the last 3 months).

Will I get a registered office address? ▼

Yes — Silver and Gold packages include a registered office address in the UK. The Starter package requires you to provide your own address.

What is the Authentication Code used for? ▼

The Authentication Code is required to make changes to your company details at Companies House. It provides secure access to your company record and is essential for future updates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can non-US residents form an LLC? ▼

Yes — EPRIMEX Ltd specialises in non-resident LLC formation. You don't need to be a U.S. resident to own an LLC in most states.

What is an EIN and do I need one? ▼

EIN (Employer Identification Number) is your federal tax ID in the USA. All our packages include EIN registration, which allows you to open U.S. bank accounts and hire employees.

Which state should I choose? ▼

Wyoming and New Mexico are popular for low registration fees and business-friendly laws. Delaware is known for legal protection. We advise based on your specific business needs.

How long does formation take? ▼

Usually 5–10 business days depending on the state. Once your LLC is formed, you'll receive all documents and can immediately open a U.S. business bank account.
